About Us

Located at the heart of Vienna, the city of psychoanalysis, PsychPhil offers you the opportunity to connect with therapists specializing in the so called "Depth Psychology" schools. Freudian, Jungian, Lacanian therapists are all to be found at our clinic. We aim for the best and most effective therapies for tackling neurosis and do not take the 'quick fixes' of the superficial cognitive therapies as long-term solutions. Get in touch with us to find out more.

  • Specializing in 'Deep Therapies' for long-term problem resolution.
  • No reliance on quick-fixes.
